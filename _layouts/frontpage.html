Academic Excellence Award - 2018

Every year the Au Co Vietnamese Cultural Center and the Academic Awards Foundation recognizes students of Vietnamese descent who have excelled academically in an annual awards ceremony.
At the end of the academic school year, qualified students from San Francisco schools and other schools in the surrounding area attend this momentous event which honors their academic achievements in front of friends and family.
