42 Years of Vietnamese Refugee Resettlement

This public cultural event will feature an exhibition and program to mark the 42nd anniversary of the Vietnamese diaspora and refugee resettlement. This exhibition shares the refugee experience and celebrates the achievements of the Vietnamese people worldwide through photography, first-person narratives, and historical artifacts.
